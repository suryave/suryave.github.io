About AMUNC

AMUNC is the first Model United Nations conference hosted by the Middlesex County Academy for Science, Mathematics, and Engineering Technologies in Edison, New Jersey.

Created by passionate and talented delegates who have both attended local and national conferences, AMUNC is designed to be a friendly learning experience to beginning MUN delegates but also as competetive and fully fledged as any national or international conference.
