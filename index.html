About Me

I'm a high school sophomore at the Middlesex County Academy for Science, Mathematics, and Engineering Technologies, and I love science and programming. I'm a student researcher at the Waksman Institute for Microbiology in association with the Rutgers DNA Sequence Analysis Program, and one of the lead web developers for Websilon, an interactive online mathematics game to teach math concepts to younger children. In my free time, I learn new programming languages, read books, and do some freelance work.
